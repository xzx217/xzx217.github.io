About Me

I am a third-year PhD student at MIT. I am very fortunate to be advised by Virginia Vassilevska Williams and Lisa Sauermann. Previously, I graduated from MIT with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science.

My main research interest is in extremal combinatorics and combinatorial algorithms.
